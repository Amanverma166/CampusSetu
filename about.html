Welcome To CampusSetu

“Campus ka raasta, seedha yahan se.”

Campus Setu is a trusted name in education and career guidance, committed to bridging the gap between ambition and achievement. Located in Nangloi, Delhi, we specialize in offering university-recognized degree programs such as B.Ed, M.Ed, JBT, B.Pharmacy, D.Pharmacy, and DMLT, in collaboration with reputed institutions like MDU Rohtak and PGIMS.

In addition to academic courses, we empower students with job-ready computer training in areas like Digital Marketing, Graphic Designing, AutoCAD, Tally, DCA, and more — designed to match today’s industry needs.

With a focus on affordability, authenticity, and assured placement support, Campus Setu is more than just an institute — it’s a launchpad for your future. Our mission is simple: to provide quality education, practical skills, and real career opportunities under one roof.

  • Trusted education partner for thousands of students
  • Affordable fee structure starting from ₹30,000/year
  • 100% Placement Guarantee on selected programs
  • Courses designed for 12th pass and graduates
  • Prime location: Najafgarh Road, Nangloi, Delhi
